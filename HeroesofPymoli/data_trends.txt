Heroes of Pymoli Data Trends

1. The vast majority of players are male (84%) and they account for the most purchases.

2. However,the average purchase amounts for males are less than those for females.

3. Roughly half (45%) of the players are aged between 20-24 years old; not surprisingly, players aged 20-24 
	account for the greatest number of purchases.

4.  Final Critic and Oathbreaker and Last Hope of the Breaking Storm were the two items
	purchased the most, also resulting in those two items being the most profitable.
